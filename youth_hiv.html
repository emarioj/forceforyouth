HIV/AIDS prevention

HIV/AIDS prevention, in partnership with AHF Uganda Cares, FORCE mobilized over 200 youths and community members for HIV testing during the International AIDS Day at Arapai market grounds in Soroti City. The organization also actively supported Sensitization to highrisk groups during International AIDS Day

Currently, FORCE collaborates with the Aids Health Care Foundation (AHF) to strengthen young people's engagement in HIV prevention through the STEP Project. The organization also mobilizes young girls for the Girls Act activities in Soroti and partners with TASO in mobilizing for testing and psychosocial support.
