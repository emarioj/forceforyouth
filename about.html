Who we are

Foundation for Resilience and Community Empowerment (FORCE) is an indigenous not for profit, non-partisan, an inclusive pro Youth Organization registered with serial number:6663/INDP160945585 and permit number: 8492/INDP0005585NB with NGO bureau and URSB reg No 80020000524668. FORCE is mandated to operate in Uganda.

FORCE was founded in 2017 as a saving scheme and consultancy firm by 3 young men from different fields of expertise who had the desire to bring about change in their lives and of there communities. later FORCE evolved into an NGO.

FORCE exists to bridge the gap of un inclusive participation of youth and women in leadership and governance processes, unequal access to health and health related information, youth unemployment, Sexual risk behaviours, gender-based violence, child neglect and abandonment. FORCE does this through equipping, empowering and informing the youth and other marginalized groups of persons using sustainable community-based models that propel the attainment of high level of self-reliance and prosperity among the youth and community at large.

Mission

To empower marginalized and Vulnerable communities with knowledge and skills for prosperity.
